## What's new in this version

```
Paint your clothes, don't just place them. This update adds real fill styles
and fixes the tools that weren't pulling their weight.

- New: pattern & gradient region fills. Paint any panel with a solid color, a
  linear or radial gradient, or a pattern — stripes, checker, dots, or camo.
- Patterns wrap the body. The same fill on connecting panels flows across the
  seams as one piece instead of restarting at each edge.
- Gradients span the whole area. Paint a gradient across connected panels and
  they share one smooth ramp — no visible breaks.
- Live editing. Change the angle or colors after painting and every panel
  updates at once. No re-clicking.
- The eraser works now — it erases from the selected layer, with a cursor that
  shows your brush size.
- Undo everything, and redo too (Ctrl+Y). Undo now covers region fills,
  backgrounds, and Shirt/Pants switches — not just layers.
- Smoother all around: themed scrollbars, keyboard focus outlines, and clearer
  prompts so you're never left wondering what happened.

used nominatively to describe the template file format. RBX15 Classic Shirt and
Pants Maker is not affiliated with or endorsed by Roblox Corporation.
```
